# Preprocess Gas Formulas

Generated by `gen_preprocess_table.py`. 1 gas = 1 ns on reference
hardware. Each preprocess code's cost is the slope of
`ns/op(pure)` over the per-construct visit count `N` reported by
`BenchmarkPreprocess<Code>/N=…`. The slope is the per-construct
cost: it captures the target visit plus any tightly-coupled
sibling visits the source builder produces (e.g. a struct type
alias also fires `FieldTypeExpr` and `NameExpr` visits). This
over-attribution direction is DoS-safe — production gas is
slightly higher than the physical per-visit cost.

Scale factor applied: ×1.5 (host → Xeon-equivalent).
