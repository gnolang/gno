# Partnerships

### Fund and Grants Program

Are you a builder, tinkerer, or researcher? If you're looking to create awesome
dApps, tooling, infrastructure, or smart contract libraries on Gno.land, you can
apply for a grant. The Gno.land Ecosystem Fund and Grants program provides
financial contributions for individuals and teams to innovate on the platform.

Read more about our Funds and Grants program
[here](https://github.com/gnolang/grants).
